About

Django Admin HoneyPot

A secure decoy experience that hides your real admin behind a honeypot front door while monitoring suspicious activity.

Problem

Admin URLs are prime brute-force targets. Attackers hammer the login page, consuming resources and probing for weak credentials.

Motivation

We want a lightweight, configurable deterrent that records malicious attempts, slows attackers, and keeps the real admin entry hidden.

Solution

A honeypot login at /admin/ lures probes, applies lockouts, and logs behavior, while the real admin lives safely at /secret-admin-entrance/.
